About Our Coaching

Guru's Tennis Academy was established in the year 1985 ny N Guruswamy with 20 students, with the dedication in his work he started his own court in Annanagar, Chennai by 1990. After his successfull tenure completion he has given oppurtunity to his son Mr.Venkatesh to manage and train students of Guru's Tennis Academy. Mr. Venkatesh who has completed his state and district level matches in India and got an international oppurtunity to coach Junior and Senior students in Muscat and Oman between 2003 and 2015.

We have a completely modernized approach to coaching, recommended by the ITF. We are qualified to address not only the technical and tactical areas of the sport but also the physical and psychological areas. We update ourselves with the latest developments in the sport by attending the ITF worldwide coach's workshop and the Asian coach's workshop, representing India through the AITA. Regarding the beginner kids program, our approach is based on maximum activity with maximum fun along with learning the right basics. The advanced training program for tournament players and future kids will compulsorily include regular fitness training session and periodical psychological sessions. The fitness training includes individualized fitness programming based on periodical fitness tests. We also have, in our employ, a fitness trainer who specializes in fitness training, specific for tennis. We take immense pride in producing champions across all levels. GTS tennis has always been synonymous with world-class talents. 

Philosophy

We at firmly believe there is no substitute for the three D's in life- Discipline, Dedication and Determination. Despite our 'tough as nails' approach towards this beautiful game, we have created a platform for all players to enjoy their game. Needless to say, this makes us a cut above the rest. At the end of the day, tennis is a test of skill and will but not war.

Vision and Mission

Our vision is to channelize the energy of the young players, tune-up their skills and evolve them into well-rounded tennis pro’s both physically and mentally with absolutely no compromise on 100% performance.

Our mission is to provide state-of-the-art facilities and astute coaching to instill in the players the technique and temperament to achieve success at the highest level.
